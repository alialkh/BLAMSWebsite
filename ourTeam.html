Our Story

Founded by Agnes Ameh in 2023, our commitment to making a difference in the lives of people/families is at the heart of our practice. We understand the profound importance of family unity and offer unwavering support, whether you're facing family challenges or seeking legal solutions in areas like family & child welfare, immigration, criminal law, real estate, and wills and estate planning.

We know that life's legal challenges can be multifaceted, and our mission is to guide and protect you in every aspect of your legal journey. At AAMEH LAW FIRM, your story is our story, and together, we can create a brighter chapter for you and your loved ones. No matter the legal challenges you face, you can trust AAMEH LAW FIRM to be your dedicated legal partner.

Agnes Ojone Ameh

Principal Lawyer
LL.B., B.A.
Since graduating from Nnamdi Azikiwe University in 2002 in Nigeria and her subsequent call to the bar of Alberta in 2018, Agnes has supported hundreds of people through a wide variety of areas of law. These areas include parenting/child custody, Guardianship, child/spousal support matters, separation & divorce, immigration/refugee law, criminal law, real-estate/ wills & estate management.

Agnes draws on her international background to bring a unique perspective to her areas of practice. She's a dedicated advocate for her clients, having spent years supporting both men and women through a variety of different challenging legal matters. Her unwavering commitment to clients and her breadth of legal acumen empowers her clients and protect their interests.

Overall, Agnes's tireless advocacy and long record of expertise make her an invaluable asset to anyone seeking resolution and support in a wide range of legal domains.
