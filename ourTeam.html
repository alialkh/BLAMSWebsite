Our Story

Founded by Agnes Ameh in 2023, our commitment to making a difference in the lives of families is at the heart of our practice. We understand the profound importance of family unity and offer unwavering support, whether you're facing family challenges or seeking legal solutions in areas like real estate, immigration, criminal law, or wills and estate planning.

We know that life's legal challenges can be multifaceted, and our mission is to guide and protect you in every aspect of your legal journey. At AAMEH LAW FIRM, your family's story is our story, and together, we can create a brighter chapter for you and your loved ones. No matter the legal challenges you face, whether it's a family crisis, real estate transaction, immigration matter, criminal defense, or planning for the future, you can trust AAMEH LAW FIRM to be your dedicated legal partner.

Agnes Ojone Ameh

Principal Lawyer
LL.B., B.A.
Since graduating from Nnamdi Azikiwe University in 2002 in Nigeria with an LL.B. and B.A., Agnes has supported hundreds of people through child Custody, Gaurdianship, child/spousal support matters, divorce and division of property matters.

Agnes draws on her international background to bring a unique perspective to Family Law. She's a dedicated advocate for her clients, having spent years supporting both men and women through challenging divorces and intricate family matters. Her unwavering commitment to clients empowers them to attain their goals and protect their interests.

Agnes is a remarkable individual who has championed the rights of children and families in the complex realm of child welfare law and guardianship matters. With a wealth of experience and unwavering dedication, she stands as a beacon of hope and support for those navigating the often intricate and emotionally charged landscape of family law. Her profound knowledge of the legal intricacies surrounding child custody, foster care, adoption, and guardianship is matched only by her compassion and empathy for her clients.

Agnes also brings a wealth of knowledge in other areas, including immigration and refugee law, criminal law, and wills and estate planning. In immigration and refugee law Agnes has been instrumental in helping individuals and families find their path to a new home and secure legal status. Additionally, her dedication extends to criminal law, where she vigorously defends the rights and interests of her clients, ensuring a fair and just legal process. Furthermore, Agnes offers invaluable guidance in wills and estate planning, assisting clients in safeguarding their legacies and providing for their loved ones with a thoughtful and comprehensive approach.

Overall, her tireless advocacy and long record of expertise make Agnes an invaluable asset to anyone seeking resolution and support in a wide range of legal domains.
